# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 14, 2026**

## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Utility Cam ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Optional Descriptions**: Photo and video descriptions you add are stored locally on your device only.
- **App Settings**: Your preferences (language, theme, notification settings, etc.) are stored locally on your device.
- **Biometric Data**: If you enable the app lock feature, biometric authentication data (fingerprint/face ID) is processed and stored securely by your device's operating system. We do not have access to your biometric data.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Camera and Media**: The app requires camera access to capture photos and videos. All media is stored locally on your device.
- **Storage Access**: The app requires storage access to save and retrieve media files on your device.
- **Audio Recording**: When recording videos, the app requires microphone access to capture audio.
- **Device Information**: Basic device information may be collected for analytics (if you consent), crash reporting, and ad delivery.
- **Analytics Data**: If you consent, we collect anonymous usage statistics through Firebase Analytics to improve the app. This includes:
  - App features used (e.g., photo captured, settings changed)
  - App screens viewed
  - General device information (OS version, device model)
  - App performance and crash data
- **Advertising Data**: If you are using the free version, Google AdMob collects information to serve personalized ads. This includes:
  - Advertising ID
  - Device information
  - Interaction with ads
  - General location data (country/region)

## How We Use Information

The information collected is used for:

- **Core Functionality**: Providing camera, media management, and app features
- **User Experience**: Remembering your preferences and settings
- **App Improvement**: Understanding how users interact with the app through analytics (with your consent)
- **Monetization**: Displaying advertisements to free users through Google AdMob
- **Security**: Authenticating your identity through biometric authentication (if enabled)
- **Communication**: Triggering in-app review prompts and feedback mechanisms

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All photos, videos, descriptions, and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Upload**: We do not upload your media or personal data to our servers
- **Full Control**: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time
- **Secure Preferences**: App settings are stored using Android's secure DataStore API

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

### Google Firebase Analytics

- **Purpose**: Anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting (requires user consent)
- **Data Collected**: App events, screen views, device information, crash logs
- **Privacy Policy**: [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Control**: You can disable analytics in the app's Settings

### Google AdMob

- **Purpose**: Display advertisements to free users
- **Data Collected**: Advertising ID, device information, ad interactions, general location
- **Privacy Policy**: [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)
- **Control**: You can remove ads by upgrading to the Pro version

### Google Play Billing

- **Purpose**: Process in-app purchases (Pro upgrade)
- **Data Collected**: Purchase transactions, payment information (handled by Google Play)
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Google Play In-App Review

- **Purpose**: Request app ratings and reviews
- **Data Collected**: Review interactions (handled by Google Play)
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Google ML Kit Document Scanner

- **Purpose**: Document scanning feature
- **Data Collected**: Processed on-device only, no data sent to servers
- **Privacy Policy**: [ML Kit Privacy](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms)

- **Accept**: Enables Firebase Analytics and personalized ads
- **Decline**: Disables analytics; ads will still be shown but will be less relevant

## Data Sharing

We do not directly share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties. However:

- **Google Services**: Analytics and advertising data is shared with Google (Firebase, AdMob) as described above
- **No Media Sharing**: Your photos and videos are never shared with us or third parties
- **Local Only**: All media remains on your device unless you manually share it

## Pro Version

The Pro version offers an ad-free experience with additional features:

- **No Ads**: Removes all advertisements and associated data collection by AdMob
- **Privacy**: Eliminates advertising tracking while still respecting your analytics consent choice
- **One-Time Purchase**: No subscriptions or recurring charges

## Biometric Authentication

If you enable the app lock feature:

- **Local Processing**: All biometric authentication is handled by your device's secure hardware
- **No Access**: We cannot access your fingerprint or face data
- **System API**: Uses Android's standard BiometricPrompt API
- **Optional**: This feature is completely optional and can be disabled anytime

## Notifications

The app may send notifications for:

- **Cleanup Reminders**: Notifications when photos are about to expire (can be disabled)
- **Deletion Confirmations**: Notifications when media is automatically deleted (can be disabled)

You can control notifications in Settings or through your device's system settings.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect new features or legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible to you
- **Delete**: Delete your data at any time through the app or your device's storage settings
- **Control**: Enable or disable analytics and advertising features
- **Revoke**: Revoke app permissions through your device settings
- **Export**: Share your media files through standard Android sharing mechanisms
- **Opt-Out**: Disable personalized ads through device settings (Google Advertising ID)

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- **Camera**: To capture photos and videos
- **Microphone**: To record audio for videos
- **Storage** (Android 9 and below): To save and access media files on your device
- **Notifications**: To send cleanup reminders and deletion confirmations
- **Biometric/Fingerprint**: To authenticate app access (optional, only if you enable app lock)
- **Internet**: To load advertisements and send analytics data (if consented)
- **Billing**: To process Pro version purchases through Google Play

Each permission is used only for its stated purpose and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

## Data Retention

- **Media Files**: Automatically deleted based on your configured TTL (Time To Live) settings, or manually by you
- **Preferences**: Stored until you uninstall the app
- **Analytics**: Retained by Google Firebase according to their data retention policies
- **Ad Data**: Retained by Google AdMob according to their data retention policies

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

- **Local Storage**: Media and preferences stored using Android's secure storage APIs
- **Encrypted Preferences**: Settings stored using DataStore with app-private access
- **Biometric Security**: Optional app lock using device's secure biometric hardware
- **No Server Storage**: No media or personal data transmitted to external servers
