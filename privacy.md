# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 16, 2026**

## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Utility Cam ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- We do not collect any personal information unless explicitly provided by you.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Camera Access**: The app requires camera access to capture photos and videos. Images and videos are stored locally on your device.
- **Storage Access**: The app requires storage access to save and retrieve media files on your device.
- **Device Information**: Basic device information may be collected for app functionality and crash reporting.

## How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for:

- Providing core app functionality (camera and media management)
- Improving app performance and user experience
- Debugging and crash analysis

## Data Storage

- All photos and videos are stored locally on your device
- We do not upload your media to external servers
- You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through your device's file manager

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's repository or support channels.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data (all stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data at any time
- Revoke app permissions through your device settings

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

- **Camera**: To capture photos and videos
- **Storage**: To save and access media files

Each permission is used only for its stated purpose and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.
